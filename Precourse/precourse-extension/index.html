Welcome to Pigeon Pals! Here we discuss everything great about pigeons, from the humble street pigeon to the esteemed pet pigeon.

Why Pigeons?

Pigeons often get a bad rap from people for their perceived "dirtiness" and their constant presence in urban areas, but pigeons are actually beautiful creatures!
Here are some nifty facts about pigeons.


  • They help keep insect populations in check!
  • Most pigeons you see on the street were descended from domesticated pigeons!
  • Their amazing homing ability made them invaluable messengers during wartime!
